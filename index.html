Semantics and Linguistic Theory (SALT) is the premier North American conference on semantics as a part of linguistic theory. The 30th SALT will be hosted by Cornell University on April 24 – 26, 2020. The conference will be preceded by a satellite workshop on focus on Thursday, April 23.

For questions or comments, please contact the program committee. By attending, you agree to abide by the Code of Conduct.

Invited speakers

  • Veneeta Dayal (Yale)
  • Dilip Ninan (Tufts)
  • Judith Tonhauser (OSU/Stuttgart)
  • Bernhard Schwarz (McGill)
