The proceedings of SALT are published by the LSA. The editors for SALT30 are Joseph Rhyne, Kaelyn Lamp, Nicole Dreier, and Chloe Kwon. They can be reached at salt-mailbox@cornell.edu.

Proceedings Deadline

Papers will be accepted from the final day of the conference, August 20, until September 30, 2020.

Submission Information

All presenters at SALT30 may publish in the proceedings. An email will be sent to authors by the editors, but some information is below.

The page limit is 18 pages for talks and posters (24 pages for invited talks). This does not include references, but does include all other material.

Further information concerning the submission and copyediting process for the proceedings can be found here. Additionally, the author information packet, including a LaTeX template and a style guide, can be downloaded here.
